Canada Facts

Fact #1

The Bay of Fundi in the eastern part of Canada has the highest tides in the world. The waves there are more than thirteen metres high.

Fact #2

Winters can be very cold in Canada with temperatures dropping below −40 °C (−40 °F) in some parts of the country.

Fact #3

There are more than 2,800 of hockey rinks in Canada. However, almost twice as many kids under 14 years play soccer than hockey. Ice-Hockey is still the second most practised sport in Canada for all over 15 years of age. Ice-hockey comes after golf, but before soccer!
